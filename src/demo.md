# Live Demo

Want to see Botmarley before installing anything? There's a public demo you can click around right now:

**[botmarley-demo.lipinski.work](https://botmarley-demo.lipinski.work)**

No sign-up, no licence key, no install. It's open to anyone.

## What you can do there

- Browse the dashboard and see how sessions and PnL are laid out.
- Open the strategy editor and read the built-in strategies.
- Run a backtest against historical data and read the results: equity curve, trade list, stats.
- Look at the market data charts with indicators.

## What it is and isn't

The demo is a sandbox. It's a separate instance with its own data, completely independent of any real deployment, and it isn't wired to anyone's exchange account or funds. It's there to show you the interface and let you try a backtest, not to trade.

It may reset from time to time, so don't treat anything you do there as permanent.

## Ready for the real thing?

When you want your own instance, with your own strategies and your own keys, head to [Installation](getting-started/installation.md).
